Introduction

Deal Memo Studio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Google Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your name and profile information

  • Your Google Account ID
  • Any personal information you've made publicly available on your Google Account

Google Services Data

With your explicit consent, we access:

  • Google Drive Files: We can see, edit, create, and delete all of your Google Drive files to provide document management functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide secure access to our application
  • Enable core functionality including document viewing, editing, and management
  • Process and display your Google Drive files and Google Docs documents within our application
  • Maintain your session and user preferences
  • Provide customer support when requested

Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

Data Retention

  • We only retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us
  • We automatically delete inactive accounts after 12 months of inactivity

Your Rights and Choices

Access Control

  • You maintain full control over your Google account permissions
  • You can revoke our access to your Google services at any time through your Google Account settings
  • You can request a copy of the data we have collected about you

Data Deletion

  • You can request complete deletion of your account and associated data
  • Upon account deletion, we will remove all your personal information from our systems within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes as required by law

Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Data Selling

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes

Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent

Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Deal Memo Studio's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

Limited Use Commitment

  • We only request access to Google user data that is necessary for our application to function
